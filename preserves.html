Field Notes

Cedar Run — Planted 400 native willow whips along the run. Beaver activity confirmed within five days. The wetland is rebuilding itself faster than we could.

Quill Ridge — Annual easement monitoring complete. Baseline photo points re-shot. No violations. Vulture roost count up 18%.

Saltmarsh Reach — 220 lbs of derelict crab pot ("ghost gear") pulled from the channel. Two seal entanglements prevented.

Thornfield — Pollinator meadow now self-seeding. "Mara" the gray fox photographed denning. Release counts as permanent.

Stewardship — Endowment for The Understory fully funded. The promise on that parcel is now paid for, forever.

Preserves, acreages, and residents shown here are illustrative of the model for an organization in formation, not a register of currently held land.

Cedar Run Preserve — Properties

Cedar Run Preserve

620 acres · fee-simple · acquired via bargain sale

A degraded ranch creek bought cheap and let go. We pulled the failing levees, planted willow, and stepped back. Beavers did the rest. Now it's our primary release site for waterfowl, turtles, and small mammals — the animals are released into habitat the trust will hold forever.

Endowment: funded · Monitoring: quarterly · Public access: guided only

Open Hollow — Properties

Open Hollow

410 acres · conservation easement

A fourth-generation family still ranches here. They gave up the right to subdivide and develop, forever; we hold and defend that restriction against every future owner. Proof you can protect land without owning it. Grassland raptors thrive in the un-fragmented sweep.

Endowment: funded · Monitoring: annual baseline re-shoot

Thornfield — Properties

Thornfield

340 acres · fee-simple

A failed hay operation reseeded to native pollinator meadow and oak savanna. Doubles as lifelong sanctuary pasture for non-releasable grazers and the release site where "Mara" the gray fox went wild again.

Endowment: funded · Restoration: year 4 of 10

Saltmarsh Reach — Properties

Saltmarsh Reach

260 acres · fee-simple · tidal estuary

Shorebird rehab-and-release plus a base for pulling derelict fishing gear ("ghost gear") out of the channel. The marsh we protect is the marsh we release birds back into.

Endowment: funded · Ghost gear removed: 220 lbs & counting

The Understory — Properties

The Understory

140 acres · fee-simple · old-growth fragment

Bought specifically so a ~900-bat colony and a vernal-pool amphibian community could not be logged. The neglected-taxa work nobody funds — which is exactly why we do it.

Endowment: fully funded · Access: research only

Quill Ridge — Properties

Quill Ridge

70 acres · conservation easement · the first parcel

Where Wildhaven began: one ridge, one easement, one funded endowment, one promise kept. Home to a vulture roost and our education residents "Judge" and "Quill." Small. Permanent. Proof of concept.

Endowment: funded · Status: protected in perpetuity
